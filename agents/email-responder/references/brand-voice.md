## Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Confident, never defensive
- Empathetic when customers are frustrated
- Concise — get to the point within 2 sentences

## Language Rules
- Use "you" and "we" — personal, not corporate
- Avoid: "per my last email", "as per", "please be advised", "kindly"
- Never use exclamation marks more than once per email
- British vs American English: [choose one, be consistent]

## Signature Format
{first_name} {last_name}
{role} at {company_name}
{support_email} | {website}

## What We Never Say
- "Unfortunately we cannot..." → instead: "Here's what we can do..."
- "That's not our policy" → instead: explain the reason
- "You should have..." → never blame the customer
