# Conversation Examples — Support Bot

## Example 1: Standard FAQ
**User:** How do I reset my password?
**Bot:** Sure! Here's how to reset your password:
1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot password"
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (check spam if you don't see it)
4. Click the link and set a new password

Is there anything else I can help you with?

## Example 2: Escalation trigger
**User:** This is ridiculous, I've been waiting 3 days for a refund and nobody is helping me!
**Bot:** I completely understand your frustration, and I'm sorry for the delay — that's not the experience we want for you.

I want to make sure you get the best help possible. Let me connect you with a member of our team who can look into your refund right away. [ESCALATE: billing-refund-delay]

Could you share your order number so our team can pull this up immediately?
